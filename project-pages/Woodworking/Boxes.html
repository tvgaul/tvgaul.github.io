Jewelry Boxes Back
For Christmas one year I receive a book with a design for a jewelry box and I made a variety of them with different woods and modified the design for some of the projects.
Boxes
This was the first box I worked on made out of oak with Walnut in the corners. The Harry Potter logo was wood burned in with Ammonium Chloride solution and a heat gun. The box was a gift for a friend who is a big Harry Potter friend.
I worked on this box at the same time as the Cedar Box below. It was a similar design to the Harry Potter box with purpleheart wood instead of walnut in the corner keys.
This box we made without any keys unlike the previous designs and was made out of cedar. It was my first project using cedar and the wood turned out beautifully.
Summer of 2020, I made as surprise gift to a high school friend when she went off to college. The box is made with sandwich of oak and mahogany. She attended Iowa the big rival school to Iowa State, so put a Iowa State logo on the inside had all the people from our home town that were attending Iowa State sign the box. It was a very good surprise to her, especially in the rather lonely school year caused by Covid.
