End Tables Back
Over winter break 2023, 2024 my dad and I worked of two end tables my great grandparents old house. They are made out of size quarter oak and stained fruitwood. The thick wood ensured we maintained making heavy furniture. We also turned the knobs which turned out well. The drawers are made up of maple in one and poplar in the other beings that what we had on hand. We ended up having an amazing joint across the two boards that make up the table top.
