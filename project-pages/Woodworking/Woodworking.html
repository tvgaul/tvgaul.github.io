Woodworking
When I was in middle school my family got my grandpa's woodworking tools when my grandparents choose to downsize since then I have loved learning and woodwoking with my dad and have worked on a wide variety of projects from pens to furniture. In all of our projects we come up with the design ourselves, in a few cases starting from a design we bought or found online then altering it but in the vast majority of cases came up with the designs as we went.
