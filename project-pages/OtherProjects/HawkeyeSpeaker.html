Hawkeye Speaker Back
I had a bluetooth speaker that was broken but instead of getting rid of it I decided to fix it. To get to the circuitry I had to break open the case so I gave myself the project of designing a new case. While I was at it I completely changed the shape and changed the LEDs that were powered by the board. In my redesign I changed the speaker out and added an RGB led strip that I selected a yellow color by putting resistors in to adjust the color values. I redesigned the case in Autodesk Inventor and put int a light defusing panel similar to that used in my backlit signs. I made it Hawkeye as a gift for my girlfriend.
