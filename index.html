About Me
I come from Pella, Iowa, and graduated from Pella Community High School in 2020. I am now a computer engineering senior at Iowa State University, graduating in May 2024. I plan to start a concurrent Masters in January 2024 and graduate with a Master’s of Engineering in May 2025.
Throughout my time at Iowa State, I have been active with the Solar Car, exposing me to electrical hardware design, electrical system debugging, project management, embedded systems, and team leadership. Outside campus, I learned about electrical harness design, electrical manufacturing, and testing and firmware through internships at Weiler, IBM, and a Co-Op at Collins Aerospace.
